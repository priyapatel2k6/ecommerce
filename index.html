Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a popular South Asian dessert made of deep-fried dough balls soaked in sugar syrup flavored with cardamom and rose water. It is soft, sweet, and often enjoyed at festivals and celebrations.

Kaju-katli

Kaju Katli is a popular Indian sweet made from cashew nuts, sugar, and ghee, shaped into thin diamond slices. It has a smooth, melt-in-your-mouth texture and is often enjoyed during festivals and celebrations.

Rasgulla

Rasgulla is a soft, spongy Indian dessert made from balls of chhena (curdled milk) cooked in light sugar syrup. It's known for its light texture and juicy sweetness, commonly enjoyed during festivals.

Laddo

Besan ke Laddoo is a traditional Indian sweet made from roasted gram flour (besan), ghee, and sugar, shaped into round balls. It's rich, nutty, and often flavored with cardamom and garnished with nuts

Kheer

Kheer is a traditional Indian dessert made by simmering rice, milk, and sugar together. It's often flavored with cardamom, saffron, and garnished with nuts, and is commonly prepared during festivals and special occasions.
